REQUIRED_VAR [line 36]

&mixed &REQUIRED_VAR( &$var, [string $varname = null], &mixed $var)

Thows {@see VariableRequiredException) if !$var (http://ru2.php.net/manual/ru/types.comparisons.php).

In constructor of VariableRequiredException passed object(backtrace). Otherwise return ref to var (&ref). This is usefull in direct operations like assigment, or other. F.e: $this->settings = REQUIRED_VAR($settings);
