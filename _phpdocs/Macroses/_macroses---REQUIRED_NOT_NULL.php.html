&mixed &REQUIRED_NOT_NULL( &$var, [string $varname = null], &mixed $var)

Thows {@see VariableIsNullException) if is_null($var) In constructor of VariableIsNullException passed object(backtrace).

Otherwise return ref to var (&ref). This is usefull in direct operations like assigment, or other. F.e: $this->settings = REQUIRED_VAR($settings);
